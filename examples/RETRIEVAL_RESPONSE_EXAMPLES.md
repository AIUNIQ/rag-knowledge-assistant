# Retrieval Response Examples

## Example 1 — Policy Question

User:

> What is the refund policy?

Retrieved Context:

> Refunds are available only within the approved refund window and must be verified against the customer record.

Grounded Response:

> Refunds are available within the approved refund window and require customer record verification.
